Our story

At Ludara.AI, we believe that every story can be a personal adventure. After years in R&D, creative technology, and business development, we saw a huge gap: museums, tour sites, educators, and publishers were delivering the same generic content to everyone—missing the chance to truly connect. We built Ludara.AI to change that. Our platform transforms any existing content into deeply personalized, gamified narratives—crafted for each visitor’s background, interests, and emotional triggers. Whether you’re walking through a historic city, exploring an art exhibit, or learning online, Ludara.AI turns static information into an interactive journey that feels made just for you. By blending AI-driven personalization with storytelling and game mechanics, we help cultural institutions, tourism providers, and educational platforms captivate audiences, drive repeat visits, and create lasting emotional bonds.

Our first product, A Perfect Day, brings this vision to hotels through interactive, locally-curated city guides. More products are on the way.
