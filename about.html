Our story

We started Ludara to help teams make better, faster decisions with AI they can trust. After years building data products and ML systems, we saw organizations struggle to connect insights to action. Ludara bridges that gap with secure data flows, explainable reasoning, and human approvals—so the best decision is also the easiest one.
