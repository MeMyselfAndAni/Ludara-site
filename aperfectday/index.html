What This Guide Is

Everything your guests need.
Nothing they have to figure out.

Interactive map with filters

Filterable by neighbourhood, activity type and opening hours. Guests find exactly what they're looking for, guided by your concierge's knowledge.

Works offline — no app needed

Guests save the map once on hotel WiFi. The full guide then works completely offline. No app to install, no data required while exploring.

Fully branded for your hotel

Your colours, your logo, your recommendations — experienced by guests as a hotel service, not a third-party platform.

Place cards reflecting your standards

Every place includes a photo, description, opening hours, and your concierge's personal note. Only places you approve appear.

Multi-lingual

Available in the languages your international guests read. We handle all translation — you share one link, it adapts to the reader.

Live in 2 weeks

We research, build, host and maintain the entire guide. Your team approves the place list. Then you share via QR code, email, or your hotel website.

For Your Concierge & Guests

A tool for every moment of the stay.

From the welcome email before arrival to the PDF they keep forever.

Before arrival

Send each guest a personalised itinerary — as an interactive link or printable PDF — before they land, tailored to their interests and the length of their stay.

At the desk & during the stay

Concierge pulls up the live map at check-in to build a day plan together. Guests carry the same guide throughout the city, adjusting dynamically.

Standard day trips

Pre-built itineraries ready to share in one tap. Always current, always on-brand. Perfect for guests with limited time.

Personalised itineraries

Create bespoke routes in seconds and share as a PDF or live link. Guests save favourites and generate a printable day guide to keep forever.
